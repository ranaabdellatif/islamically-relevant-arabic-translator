Islamically-Relevant Arabic Transliterator

This tool was created for the purpose of unifying the ummah. Many Muslims who do not speak Arabic may feel alienated due the common use of Arabic word within the Muslim community. This tool aims to help aid these Muslims or non-Arabic speakers with understanding the Islamic terminology. The tool provides the direct translation of the Arabic word into English, a detailed explanation of the context of the word, and finally, an example of the word being used in a setence of Islamic context.
